What I Do

Hi, I'm Emily... a creative gal out here just trying to spread my passion for funky and free-flowing, raw and wildly untamed artwork. My inspiration and ideas come from the gifts provided from the grand human experience. My intention is to capture weird phrases and make them look artsy, to create funky unprofessional graphics and to allow my watercolor paintings to flow in order to discover their unique beauty. My artwork allows me the freedom to express my authenticity and to hopefully inspire others to do the same.
