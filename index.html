What I Do

Hi, I'm Emily! I am a creative just trying to spread my passion for funky and free flowing, raw and wildly untamed and enigmatic artwork. My artwork highlights the unique essence of my being as well as the stunning environment I have been surrounded by here on the Central Coast.
I'm so excited to share a little piece of me with you!

Who I Am

Just an average Joe out here trying to spread some joy and creativity through my authentic kooky visual expressions. I've been doing art for as long as I can remember and recently have realized that it is a passion of mine that I'd like to pursue and create more of.
I hope you enjoy!

My Work

I dabbled around with watercolors, graphic design and occasional mural artwork. I have done most of my work for friends and for fun, and enjoy having the freedom and flexibility to see what flows. My art is everchanging and I can ensure that if you visit this website frequently, there will always be a fresh piece to share with you!
